## 2. Weekly Fundraising Plan

### Week 1-2: Initial Outreach
1. **Cold Emails (50-100 investors)**
   - Use Email Template 1
   - Attach one-pager
   - Track open rates
   - A/B test subject lines

2. **Follow-ups**
   - Send Email 2 after 3 days
   - Focus on new traction/metrics
   - Suggest specific meeting times

### Week 3-4: Warm Introductions
1. **Target: 10-15 warm intros**
   - Use Email Template 5
   - Leverage mutual connections
   - Provide forwardable email copy

2. **Meeting Preparation**
   - Customize pitch deck
   - Prepare customer references
   - Set up data room access

### Week 5-6: Meetings & Due Diligence
1. **Meeting Strategy**
   - Present 5-slide deck live
   - Focus on metrics and traction
   - Send Email 6 post-meeting
   - Share additional materials as requested

2. **Due Diligence Support**
   - Provide data room access
   - Schedule customer calls
   - Share detailed financial model

### Week 7-8: Closing
1. **Create Urgency**
   - Use Email Template 4
   - Share real traction updates
   - Set clear deadlines
   - Get verbal commitments

2. **Close the Round**
   - Target 15-20% success rate
   - Aim for 8-12 week close
   - Coordinate term sheet reviews
